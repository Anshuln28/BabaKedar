Sagittarius

Ruling Planet:
Jupiter

Represented By:
Archer

Type:
Fire

Characterstics:
Visionary, bountiful, real explorer, faithful and entertaining.

Sagittarius have a great energy level and they are the most enthusiastic personalities. They love travelling and spending money frequently more than any other sign. Its representatives are philosophical in nature and love to view humanity from different angles. They are highly social people and love travelling. They give their best to accomplish their projects. They are good at bringing their thoughts into action. Kind and considerate, they are deeply loyal towards their family and people around them and its is impossible for them not to act when someone is in need.

Ruled by the Jupiter which is the biggest among all other planets, people represented by this sun sign have extreme inquisitive with an appreciable sense of humour. They can become great researcher, master of any art, a professional photographer or a successful exporter. They love to explore and travel.

Sagittarians love to be free from any restrictions and are fond of exploring and inventing other lifestyles or traditions. People referring to this sign, are joyful and like to spend productive time with their friends and near and dear ones. Such people are trustworthy and committed to their life partners. They also expect their loved ones to be soft hearted and interactive with them all the time. They have a pleasing personality and termed as the happiest sun sign among all. They are quite honest and hence become impulsive sometimes and lose the opportunity.

Behavior:
Sagittarians are flexible and visionary with high on imagination. They are analytical and open to creative as well as innovative ideas and theories. Such individuals have plethora of friends with whom they have fun, share the multiplicity of culture, music and various standards of life. They are focused about their family members too and can go out of the way to do their best when needed.

Whom Sagittarius should avoid:
Scorpions should avoid Cancerians and Taurians in order to keep themselves away from conflicts and unwanted debates with no conclusion.

Best Compatible sign:
Aries are the most compatible sun sign and a superb match for Sagittarius. They both make an excellent duo because of their adventurous behaviour and style of exploring things. Their energy and excitement level is similar and hence they attract each other. Gemini, Leo and Aquarius are also beneficial zodiacs who give good results for Sagittarians.

Lucky Gemstone for Sagittarius women:
Yellow sapphire is the luckiest stone for Sagittarius women as it brings positivity and success in their lives. This stone also enhances overall personality and prosperity as well. Women are advised to wear the original legitimate gemstone of a good quality rather than any alternative of it.

Lucky Gemstone for Sagittarius men:
Turquoise is the lucky gemstone for men as it brings positive energy and fortune in life. There are other variants like Ruby, Citrine, Topaz and Blue Zircon which also give effective results.

Lucky Color:
Blue is the most favourable colour for Sagittarius as it sustains happiness for a long time. There is another option, Purple, which is also good for this zodiac.

Lucky Number:
Number 3 or total of this number like 12, 21 and 30 are beneficial ones for Sagittarians. There are other numbers like 9, 42 and 7 also give good results.
