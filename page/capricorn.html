Capricorn

Ruling Planet:
Saturn

Represented By:
Goat

Type:
Earth

Characterstics:
Well organized, self-restraint, punctual, ability to manage things and accountable.

Capricorn is a sign of utmost responsibility and punctuality towards everything. Its representatives are usually conservative and sincere by birth. They are good managers and organize everything as planned. Capricorns are experts in planning and managing superb and logical plans.

As it is the element of earth, its representatives are practical and humble in their approach. They have an ability to handle several people at one time to get the task accomplished. The biggest setback is that such people become adamant and are not ready to accept others’ ideas or styles.

This sun sign allows its people to be the finest decision maker and give them a power to take initiatives with an aim to prevent the devil creates trouble in their minds. Capricorns keep their secrets with them and confront the world with a fear. Although they are scared of their devils however they never run away from the people or situations.

Capricorns don’t forgive people for their mistakes and when they are irritated badly, they begin hating everything and everyone.

Behavior:
Capricorns are well organized and self disciplined personality who understands the value of time management and people management very well. They are intellectuals, loyal and followers of customs and rituals by heart. It’s very hard to attain a Capricorn’s heart as they are hard nut to break but once they get involved in the relationship, there’s no looking back for them. They prove to be the great friends and family members.
As Saturn is its ruling planet, its individuals put instructions in every work and it becomes difficult to make them understand at times. They are fond of handicraft work of high quality and various genres of music. At some situations, they become arrogant and it’s really hard for them to accept someone else’s perspectives as they impose their old rigid ones.

Whom Capricorn should avoid:
Capricorns should stay away from Cancerians to avoid any type of dissatisfaction and mismanagement.

Best Compatible sign:
Taurus, Cancer and Scorpio are the most compatible sun signs for Capricorns as they maintain a stable relationship and positive energy. Virgo, Pisces and Capricorn also show good engagement.

Lucky Gemstone for Capricorn women:
Garnet is the most effective gemstone for Capricorn women as it brings great fortune and enhancement in prosperity. There are other choices of stones too, for instance; Blue Topaz, Ruby and Agate which also give better results.

Lucky Gemstone for Capricorn men:
The perfect gemstones for Capricorn men are Blue sapphire and Garnet. Both these stones increase their efficiency and overall performance to deliver the best of their abilities. Capricorn men can be benefitted from other gemstones like Lapis lazuli, Blue topaz, Onyx and Ruby.

Lucky Color:
Black is the most beneficial colour for Capricorns in every aspect. There are also options of other variants like brown, green and purple which provide positive energy.

Lucky Number:
8 is the luckiest number for Capricorns as it brings prosperity. The other numbers are 5, 4, 13 and 22 which are also influential ones.
