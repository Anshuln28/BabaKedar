Cancer

Ruling Planet:
Moon

Represented By:
Crab

Type:
Cardinal Water

Characterstics:
Determined, faithful, Influential and highly innovative.

Cancer is the most inspiring sun sign among all. People of this zodiac sign are truly focussed on their work. They are delicate and caring for their family members and support them fully. Being Moon as the ruling planet, Cancerians are impatient and get into self-pity state sometimes which makes them dull. They like to resolve their or someone else’s issues prominently.

Cancer people are proved to behave like a crab who assumes that he is walking on the right path however it is not quite often. They need to be taught lessons through hard times of life and thereafter they realize. Determination is an important tool that a Cancerian utilizes to achieve anything he/she has planned for. They also become irrational and do not behave like a practical person in some situations.

The individuals having Cancer sun sign love to spend time with their families. They enjoy the pursuit of happiness and are subtle in nature.

Cancerians like their partners to read between lines and understand their expectations with the help of some clues. Cancerians like to fabricate the truth to represent things in a different way. They like to involve new contacts and spend productive time with them however they get disappointed if their close ones do not accept the newcomers.

Behavior:
Cancerians are highly innovative and keep experimenting in household routines or work assignments. These people are the most reliable ones. If anyone shares any secret with them, they would disclose it to anybody. They are able to convince people in terms of agreeing upon their ideas and presentations. They are creative and spontaneous to help others without any profit out of it.
Cancerian confront the ones who are stronger and superior to them as they are not scared to take challenges.

Whom Cancer should avoid:
Cancer should avoid getting indulged with Sagittarius to keep themselves away from any type of friction or facing challenges in mutual understanding.

Best Compatible sign:
Scorpio, Taurus, and Virgo are the best congenial signs for Cancer as these zodiac signs prove to be the best match. Pisces also show positivity and a smooth relationship with Cancer.

Lucky Gemstone for Cancer women:
Pearl is the most productive gemstone for Cancer women as it is magical and creamy which provides complete protection abundantly. There are variants of moonstone and Emerald Stones in which women can opt for better results.

Lucky Gemstone for Cancer men:
Ruby is the most favorable gemstone for all Cancer men as it brings great luck and prosperity. It also enhances their overall personality and helps them to maintain their relationships. Ruby also proves to be truly effective for Cancer in monetary terms in a significant way. Emerald, pearl, and moonstone are also the options to wear.

Lucky Color:
Yellow, white, and red is the luckiest colors for Cancerians which help them become thrive. Cream is also an alternate option to wear for better luck.

Lucky Number:
Lucky number for Cancerians is 7 or the total of it, like 25, 52, 34, 16, 43 and 61 as they bring great destiny for them. 2 and 9 numbers are also effective for smoothening things.
