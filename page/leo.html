Leo

Ruling Planet:
Son

Represented By:
Lion

Type:
Fire

Characterstics:
Innovative, highly supportive, kind-hearted, courageous and leadership qualities.

Represented by a lion, Leos imbibe all the qualities of a lion and are considered to be born leaders in every field they undertake. They have a great thought process and a plethora of creative ideas that play an excellent role in their lives. Leos utilize their creativity within the family and at the professional front. They are kind-hearted and are ready to support anybody every time. They also have a dominating nature through which they get their work accomplished by others in a way they want.

As the ruling planet is Sun, its representatives are impassioned and genuine in their approach of life. They express their warm feelings to their partners straightaway with full clarity. They expect the same intellect and working pattern from their beloved ones. Leos have a strong commitment towards work and hence they accomplish the assignments prominently.

Behavior:
Leos are liberal and warm hearted people with an appealing personality to attract. They have numerous friends and people connected and they are faithful to them. Leos love to resolve the challenges and confront any type of obstacles with their efforts and resources. This sign has the traits of a lion and it’s almost impossible to challenge them in any circumstances. They have the tendency to win the battle, be it love or war. It’s not at all easy to defeat a Leo as they never give up.

Whom Aries should avoid:
Leos should avoid Leo as this sign which has the element of fire is emotional and the relationship between both of them may end up wasting time unnecessarily. Pisces and Libra are also least compatible with Leos.

Best Compatible sign:
Aries and Gemini are the most compatible zodiac signs for Leos as they are adventurous and have great energy level to please their partners. Sagittarius, which is also the element of fire, indicates good engagement with Leos.

Lucky Gemstone for Aries women:
Green peridot is the perfect gemstone for the Leo women as it enhances the beauty after getting fixed in the ornaments. This stone brings positive energy in life and the Leos women can do excellent changes which are beneficial for the society. The other effective options are golden topaz, ruby and tourmaline.

Lucky Gemstone for Aries men:
Peridot is a gemstone which brings growth and positivity for Leo men as well. This stone proves to be the luckiest one. Diamond, ruby and onyx are also the variants for good results.

Lucky Color:
Red and gold are considered to be the colours for Leos as they bring good luck and fortune in their lives. Orange is another option to influence them.

Lucky Number:
The lucky numbers for Leos are 4, 3, 17, 10, 19 and 44. Leos can expect good happenings by following these numbers.
