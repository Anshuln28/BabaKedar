Aries

Ruling Planet:
Mars

Represented By:
Ram

Type:
Fire

Characterstics:
Spontaneous, courageous, and energetic. Strong leadership quality.

Aries are the leaders, who love to lead the pack. Hence it is no surprise that they rule the top position among all sun signs. They are bold. They are ambitious. They are always ready to dive first in the challenging situations the life throws at them. Aries is a fire sign who leaps before they think that put Aries in trouble situations but most often they come out victorious learning the lessons of life the hard way.

They are bold, outspoken, highly optimistic. Aries are known for their excellent leadership skills and organizational abilities. They are speedy, spontaneous, competitive and the ones who always strive to be on the top in all their endeavors. They do not shy away from accepting challenges and often termed as impatient, proud, and arrogant.

They are highly temperamental and is explosive when in anger, though their emotional outbursts do not last long and very short-lived. Well, most of the times, they are highly cheerful, happy-go-lucky, positive, upbeat and playful creatures who believe in living life to the fullest. They are led with innate optimism and finding their way through life with zeal and energy complemented with distinctive impulsiveness.

Governed by ruling planet, Mars, the planet that represents valiant warriors, Aries is courageous and always ready to battle. Aries is a first and action oriented sign among all sun signs. Aries loves adventures and can be seen excelling at various sports or speeding down the highway and throwing away extravagant parties.

Behavior:
Aries has a cheerful personality and has a charm that keeps them surrounded by friends. They are motivated and confident. They have an uncomplicated and direct approach towards life and the problems. Being represented by a Ram, they are highly impulsive in nature and often driven by the relentless determination in their lives.

Whom Aries should avoid:
Whom Aries should avoid: Aries should avoid Capricorns and Cancers as these cardinal signs may get tired by their careful planning.

Best Compatible sign:
Level headed Libra may counter the impulsive characteristics of Aries and help them keeping grounded and realistic.
Aries is a symbol of an individualistic approach. They believe in each on his own and represent dare to go approach. Libra represents the partnership. The self-determined Aries are very inspiring for other signs. There are times, this trait in excess will make Aries behave in a selfish manner that may pose problems in relationships in their lives.

Lucky Gemstone for Aries women:
Diamond. Diamond is said to be the best friend for Aries’ Woman. Other lucky gemstones are jasper, red jasper, topaz, bloodstone, Sapphire.

Lucky Gemstone for Aries men:
Coral, aquamarine, bloodstone, sapphire, topaz, jasper.

Lucky Color:
Red is said to be the most auspicious color for Aries. It is a symbol of energy, power, aggressiveness, and action. Besides red, yellow and white are also good colors for Aries. The colors that Aries must avoid are blue, green, and black.

Lucky Number:
9 is the lucky Number for Aries. The numbers that added to become 9, are the important numbers for Aries like 9, 18, 27, 36, 45, 54, 63.
