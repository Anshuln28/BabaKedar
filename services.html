What We Provide

Natal Chart Analysis

Natal chart analysis is the most popular type of astrology service. It is also known as birth chart analysis. The natal chart is a map of the sky at the time of your birth. This chart is used to understand your personality, strengths, weaknesses, and potential. A natal chart analysis provides insights into your life’s purpose, career, relationships, and more. Best astrologer in India use different techniques to interpret the natal chart, including planets’ positions, aspects, and houses.

Compatibility Analysis

Compatibility analysis is a type of astrology service that examines the compatibility of two people. This analysis uses the natal charts of both individuals to determine the level of compatibility. Compatibility analysis is commonly used for romantic relationships but can also be used for business partnerships and friendships. Astrologers use different techniques to compare natal charts, including synastry and composite charts.

Forecasting

Forecasting is a type of astrology service that predicts future events. Astrologers use different techniques to forecast, including transits, progressions, and solar returns. Transits are the current positions of planets in the sky and their relationship to the natal chart. Progressions are the movement of the natal chart over time, and solar returns are the charts for the upcoming year’s birthday. Forecasting can provide insights into career opportunities, relationships, health, and more.

Election Analysis

Electional astrology is a type of astrology service that helps you choose the best time for a specific event. Astrologers use the natal chart to determine the best time for a wedding, business launch, or another significant event. Electional astrology can also be used to choose the best time to start a new project, buy a house, or travel.

Horary Analysis

Horary astrology is a type of astrology service that provides answers to specific questions. This astrology uses the time, date, and location of a question to create a chart. Astrologers use different techniques to interpret the chart and provide answers to the question. Horary astrology can be used to answer questions about careers, relationships, health, and more.

Medical Astrology

Medical astrology is a type of astrology service that examines the relationship between the natal chart and health. Astrologers use the natal chart to identify potential health issues and provide insights into treatment options. Medical astrology can be used to prevent illness, improve health, and provide guidance on the best time for medical procedures.

Financial Astrology

Financial astrology is a type of astrology service that analyzes financial trends and provides insights into investment opportunities. Astrologers use different techniques to forecast financial trends, including planetary cycles, eclipses, and fixed stars. Financial astrology can be used for personal finance or to provide insights into the stock market and global economic trends.

Mundane Astrology

Mundane astrology is a type of astrology service that predicts world events and political trends. Astrologers use different techniques to interpret the charts for countries, cities, and leaders. Mundane astrology can provide insights into the best time for business expansion, diplomatic relations, and more.

Spiritual Astrology

Spiritual astrology is a type of astrology service that helps individuals connect with their higher self and spiritual path. Astrologers use the natal chart to provide insights into past lives, karma, and spiritual purpose.



Natal Chart Analysis

If you’ve been asked “what time were you born?” and didn’t understand why, this is for you. An astrological birth chart — also referred to as the “natal chart” — uses the exact date, time, and location of your birth to re-create a snapshot of the sky at your precise moment of arrival. Within the folds of this circular map, you’ll find all the planets and celestial objects tracked in astrology, along with the zodiac signs they were occupying. Already confused? Well, it’s a lot! But fear not, my friend. Read on to explore the fundamentals of the birth chart.

Each planet represents different aspects of the individual's personality and life experiences, while the signs they occupy and the houses they fall into provide further context and nuances. Here are some key points to consider when analyzing a natal chart:

Sun Sign:
The Sun sign represents the core of one's personality and ego. It indicates the individual's basic character traits and primary motivations.

Moon Sign:
The Moon sign reflects one's emotional nature and inner self. It reveals how one instinctively reacts to and processes emotions.

Mercury, Venus and Mars:
These planets govern communication, relationships, and action, respectively. Their positions and aspects shed light on how the individual expresses themselves, forms connections, and pursues their goals.

Jupiter and Saturn:
Jupiter represents expansion, growth, and opportunities, while Saturn symbolizes discipline, responsibility, and limitations. Their placements reveal areas of potential abundance and challenges that need to be overcome through hard work and perseverance.

Uranus, Neptune, Pluto:
These planets signify transformation, spirituality, and collective evolution. Their positions indicate areas of radical change, spiritual development, and societal influence in the individual's life.

Aspects:
Aspects are the angles formed between planets, which influence their energies. Positive aspects can indicate harmony and support, while challenging aspects may suggest tension and growth opportunities.

Natal chart analysis is not about predicting specific events but rather understanding the inherent potentials and tendencies within an individual's life. It can provide valuable insights into personality traits, strengths, weaknesses, and life themes, empowering individuals to make informed choices and navigate their journey with greater self-awareness.
