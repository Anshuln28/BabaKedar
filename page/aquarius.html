Aquarius

Ruling Planet:
Uranus and Saturn

Represented By:
Water Bearer

Type:
Air

Characterstics:
Generous, innovator, Indigenous, self dependant and supportive.

Aquarians have a personality of sheer independence and generosity. On one hand, they are reserved and silent but on the other hand, they are energetic and unconventional. They possess great intellect and are excellent negotiators. They love to resolve issues. They have a talent of problem solving, despite the shortcomings, they have great ability to look for silver linings under the black clouds.

Coming under as an air sign, Aquarius people are not that emotional but pure logical and love to use their brains every now and then. They love to engage in intellectually stimulating activities, in absence of it, they are prone to get monotonous and will not be able to deliver their best. Aquarians are the most flexible individuals among all other zodiacs. Such people have the potential to do quicker and smoother modifications and hence they prove to be an excellent inventors and philosophers in life.

These people are visionary and look for the best possibilities to get the work accomplished. They are balanced characters who are well aware of where to spend money and where to save for future emergencies. They can also become amazing actors and pilots in life. They have high expectations with their partners while making love.

Behavior:
Aquarians are enthusiastic and very supportive by birth. These people like the company of their friends and also love try to spend ample of time socialising and going out and meeting new people. As its ruling planet is Uranus, people under the influence of this zodiac sign are hostile and fearful in nature however they also look for best prospects in work field. They recognize the future opportunities and are confident about their short term and long term goals.
The biggest setback with this zodiac sign is that these people are restricted and bounded by their thought process.

Whom Aries should avoid:
Scorpions should be avoided by Aquarians. It is recommended as Scorpions have a complex attitude and Aquarians get overpowered by them in any dealing.

Best Compatible sign:
The most compatible sun signs for Aquarians are Sagittarius and Gemini as they make a great match with the element Air. Leo and Libra also express positive results and engagements with Aquarians.
